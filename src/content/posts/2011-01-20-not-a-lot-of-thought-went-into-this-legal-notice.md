Not a lot of thought went into this legal notice. First, the email was sent from noreply@stupid.domain so I have no idea whom to notify. Second, you can’t return emails like you can postal mail. Once it hits the inbox and the recipient has read all the way to the end of your legal notice, there’s no turning back. I hope they come after me for disclosing, copying, distributing, and using their email for this post.
