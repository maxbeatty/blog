Forgive me, it has been almost two years since I've written. Twenty-seven year old Max is married, lives in San Francisco, and is on a journey with a small startup. My excuse for not writing last year was being too busy planning our perfect wedding. It wasn't easy, but all of the hard work paid off. All I remember is being truly happy and still am. Shortly afterwards, we moved to SF to be closer to friends and try city living while we're still young. Caitlin was hesitant without having the experience of living in Chicago, but we're both very happy with the decision. There is still so much to explore after being out in the Bay Area for 3 years. I left Reputation.com to work with John Ramey, who originally prompted me to move to Silicon Valley. In July 2012, isocket was just 10 people. Now, iSocket has officially surpassed 30. It has been an educational journey filled with new self-awareness.
