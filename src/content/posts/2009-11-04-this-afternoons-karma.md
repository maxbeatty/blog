This afternoon I ordered Chipotle online and went to pick it up. It wasn’t ready when I got there (never is when you order online). Then, as I see the lady making my delicious burrito I noticed she forgot to include rice. I could have said something and she probably would have remade it, but I didn’t. Just as I got back to my house, it started raining.

If I would have stuck around complaining, I would have had to walk home in the rain. That’s just good karma to me!
