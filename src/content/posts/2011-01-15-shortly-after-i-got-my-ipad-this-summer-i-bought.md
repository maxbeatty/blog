Shortly after I got my iPad this summer, I bought the [Twelve South Compass stand](http://twelvesouth.com/products/compass/) and love it. I received a [DODOcase for Christmas](http://www.dodocase.com/) which is equally awesome. It turns out that by using them together, I solved one of the minute imperfections of the Compass stand. If you aren’t paying attention, you can set the iPad’s volume button on the arm which results in muting it. When I have my iPad in my DODOcase, it still fits on the Compass and creates a buffer so I don’t have to worry about resting the volume button on the arm. Hooray awesome products combining forces!
