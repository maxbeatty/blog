Last Monday, friends were in town visiting. We were driving back from the city so I decided to turn on an episode of [Roderick on the Line](http://www.merlinmann.com/roderick/) for them to enjoy. Around eighteen and a half minutes into the [episode](http://www.merlinmann.com/roderick/ep-01-keep-moving-and-get-out-of-the-way.html), John nailed me.

> Being an introvert is not easy. It’s really not, and I know a lot of the people listening to this podcast are introverts because that is by definition who listens to podcasts.

Chuckle.  He continued.

> People who like to hear other people’s voices only through their car stereo speakers.

Bigger chuckle. This is oddly specific.

> Rather than having people sitting in the car with them talking to them.

Outright laughter. Spot on.
