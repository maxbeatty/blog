> **Daily chart: global malaria deaths.** The death toll from malaria seems to have responded to a big injection of money. Over the past decade deaths have fallen by 20%; of 108 countries where malaria is endemic, ten are on track to [eliminate the disease in the near future](http://www.economist.com/blogs/dailychart/2011/10/malaria).
