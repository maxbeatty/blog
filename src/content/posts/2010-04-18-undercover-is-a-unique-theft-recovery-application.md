[Undercover](http://www.orbicule.com/undercover/mac/) is a unique theft-recovery application designed from the ground up for Mac OS X. This is what I would see if my Macbook Pro was stolen along with screenshots and mugshots of the thief. If they can’t recover your laptop, they’ll give you a full refund. Why would you not have this on your Macbook or Macbook Pro? Why doesn’t Apple offer a similar service for their laptops like they do for iPhones and iPads with MobileMe?
