[staff](http://staff.tumblr.com/post/320191537/ask):

> **Now testing: Ask Me**
>
> The new Ask feature lets your friends or readers ask questions that you can choose to answer on your blog.
>
> You can enable it from your new [Messages](http://www.tumblr.com/messages) page (where we’re about to start adding a bunch of new messaging options!).
>
> They even come with a bunch of [new theme variables](http://tumblr/docs/custom_themes#answer-posts) for designers to format them:
>
> ```
> {block:AskEnabled}
>     <a href="/ask">{AskLabel}</a>
> {/block:AskEnabled}
>
> {block:Answer}
>
>         {Question}
>
>         <span class="asker">
>             <img src="{AskerPortraitURL-24}"/>
>             {Asker}
>         </span>
>
>         {Answer}
>     </div>
> {/block:Answer}
> ```
>
> Enjoy!
