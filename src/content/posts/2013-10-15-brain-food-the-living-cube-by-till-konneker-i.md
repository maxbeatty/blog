> > I moved into a apartment studio without storage room. So i made a minimalistic cube design with a shelf for my vinyl collection, my TV, Clothes and Shoes. On the cube is a guest bed and inside the cube is a lot of storage space. Remo from [www.holzlaborbern.ch](http://www.holzlaborbern.ch) transferred my sketches very beautifully. My friend and Photographer [Rob Lewis](http://www.rob-lewis.ch/) made this great photos. Info via [Behance](http://www.behance.net/gallery/The-living-cube/10280177)
