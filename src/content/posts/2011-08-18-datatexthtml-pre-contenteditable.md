> data:text/html,<pre contenteditable>

Simplest text editor ever. Just enter that into the address bar of your WebKit browser, click in the top left hand corner, and begin typing. Thanks to [Paul Irish](http://paulirish.com/) for showing everyone this trick at [Tech Talks: San Francisco](http://sftechtalks.com/).
